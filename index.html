About

Hi, I'm Victor Tan, currently pursuing a software engineering degree at the Singapore Institute of Technology. Previously a graduate from Temasek Polytechnic awarded with a diploma in Computer Engineering. I discovered my interest in computer programming and application development; therefore, I hope to acquire more skills and knowledge in my current studies to enable me to pursue a career in the information technology sector as a software engineer.
