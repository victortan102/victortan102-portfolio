Project Overview

MediMate is a mobile application designed to assist users in effectively managing their medication schedules. The app provides a personalized dashboard equipped with real-time notifications and reminders to ensure users never miss a dose. By leveraging artificial intelligence (AI) and mobile technology, MediMate offers advanced features such as predictive analytics for medication refill needs and integration with wearable health devices. The application primarily targets elderly individuals with chronic health conditions and patients managing complex medication regimens, aiming to improve their adherence to prescribed schedules.
